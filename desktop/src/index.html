Quick reference

What each verification stage does, and what the status flags mean.

Syntax & format

RFC 5322 regex check. Catches missing @, double @, illegal characters, and TLD-less domains.

Domain & MX heuristics

Detects typo domains (gmial.com, yhoo.com), placeholder TLDs, and obvious dead domains. Real DNS lookups are available in the cloud edition.

Disposable / spam-trap

Matches against a curated list of disposable mailbox providers (Mailinator, Yopmail, etc.) and known spam-trap patterns.

Role & free-provider tag

Flags role-based addresses (info@, sales@, admin@) and free providers (Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail) so you can segment campaigns.

Smart deduplication

Normalizes Gmail dot/plus aliases (j.s+promo@gmail.com ≡ js@gmail.com) and case differences before deduping.

Risk score (0–100)

Combines all signals into a single confidence score. ≥85 Valid · 50–84 Risky · <50 Invalid.
